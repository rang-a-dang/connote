# AI Statement

I use AI throughout my writing and development. This project is no exception.

To me, AI is a collaborator and an independent contributor. Depending on the task, that relationship ranges from "go do this, I trust you" to careful, line-by-line, word-by-word scrutiny.

Almost every idea gets filtered through a conversation with AI. Putting my thoughts into words forces me to clarify and sharpen them. And often, hearing those thoughts echoed back — even with enthusiastic agreement — helps me spot the gaps.

Whatever role AI plays, I view the output as my responsibility. If I trust AI when I shouldn't, that's my failure, not AI's. Any errors in reasoning or bugs I fail to catch are mine to own. I'm comfortable being judged on the result, regardless of AI's role in producing it.

Calibrating how much oversight to apply, and where, is an ongoing process. I hope I've struck the right balance.
